About our Lab

The main focus of our lab
is to study the neural basis of episodic memory, the memory for personal experiences.

We are particularly interested in understanding the precise nature of the contribution of the hippocampus and prefrontal cortex, as well as their functional interactions with associated structures.

We use a multi-technique approach, combining lesion, inactivation, and in vivo high-density single-unit recordings techniques in rats performing complex memory tasks.
